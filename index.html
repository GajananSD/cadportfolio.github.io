Spirometer

A spirometer is a medical device used to measure lung function by assessing the volume of air inhaled and exhaled, aiding in the diagnosis and management of respiratory conditions. It plays a crucial role in evaluating lung health.

Ball balancing on beam

Balancing a ball on a beam using a servo involves a feedback control system that adjusts the beam's angle in real-time to counteract the ball's movements, demonstrating precise control and stability.

Universal Joint

A universal joint (U-joint) is a mechanical connection that allows for the transmission of rotational motion between two shafts that are not in-line, enabling flexibility and angular movement.

Two axis wheel self balancing stick

The physics behind balancing a stick on two rotating wheels involves using sensors to detect the stick's tilt, then applying torque to the wheels to counteract the tilt, relying on principles of rotational dynamics and control theory.

Geneva wheel

A Geneva wheel is a mechanical device that translates continuous rotational motion into intermittent motion, commonly used in applications such as indexing and timing mechanisms. It features a driven pin that slots into recesses on a rotating wheel, causing it to advance in discrete steps.

Automatic stamping machine

An automatic stamping machine making stamps in a unison pattern efficiently produces multiple identical stamps simultaneously, ensuring precise and consistent alignment with each stamp being in perfect synchronization. This uniformity is essential for high-quality stamp production.

Extended Surface Heat Transfer Setup Design

In an experiment on heat transfer from extended surfaces, such as fins, heat conduction principles are studied to assess how the shape and size of these surfaces affect their ability to dissipate heat efficiently, crucial for improving the cooling of various engineering systems. This research helps optimize heat exchange processes in applications like radiators and electronic cooling systems.

Light Spectrometer Device

A light spectrometer device utilizing a diffraction grating disperses light into its component wavelengths, enabling measurement and analysis of spectral lines, which is vital in fields like chemistry, astronomy, and material characterization. It provides insights into the composition and properties of light sources or samples.

4-piston cylinder Assembly

A 4-piston cylinder assembly consists of four pistons within a common cylinder bore, typically used in high-performance engines to maximize power output and efficiency by increasing air intake and exhaust flow. This design enhances engine performance and provides better torque and horsepower.
